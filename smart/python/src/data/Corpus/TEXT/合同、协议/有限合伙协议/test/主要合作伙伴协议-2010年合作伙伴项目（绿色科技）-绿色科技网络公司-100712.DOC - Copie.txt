The Provider operates The China Greentech Initiative (“Initiative”) as an open, collaborative

platform of commercial, government and non-profit organizations seeking to accelerate the growth 

of greentech solutions in China. Within the Initiative, the 2010 Partner Program (“Program”) is 

designed to provide partner organizations with continuous market insights, meaningful industry 

relationships, and market and brand acceleration. The Program builds upon the success that the 

Initiative achieved in 2009, which included the development, publication and dissemination of The 

China Greentech Report 2009. The Partner desires to become a member of the Program, subject to 

the terms and conditions set forth below.
